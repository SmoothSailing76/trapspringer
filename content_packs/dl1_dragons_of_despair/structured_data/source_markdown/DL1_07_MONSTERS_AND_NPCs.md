## DRACONIANS (Dragonmen) — New Monster Type

Draconians are the basic troops of the dragonlords. Their origins are unknown to anyone in this section of Krynn. All have wings but neither the Baaz nor the Bozak can truly fly for more than one round.

### Movement Rates (All Draconians)
- **Walking:** 6"
- **Running on all fours (wings pushing):** 15" — must use all four limbs with wings free; they charge this way, carrying weapons in their teeth
- **Gliding:** 18" — can glide from any height; glide distance = 4× the height from which they launch

**Disguise capability:** Often encountered in disguise. Can conceal wings under robes; wearing large hood and mask, can pass through civilized lands as spies. Dragonlords frequently use Baaz this way just before an invasion.

**Death effect:** When a Baaz reaches 0 HP, it immediately turns into what appears to be a **stone statue**. If a PC deals the statue a blow with a melee weapon: must make a DEX check at –3 or their weapon will be **stuck in the still-hardening statue**. The statue crumbles to dust within 1–4 rounds after death, freeing any stuck weapon. While a weapon is stuck, it cannot be used. **Only the body turns to stone and crumbles — armor and weapons the Baaz carried are unaffected and may be used by others after it crumbles.**

## AGHAR (Gully Dwarves) — New Monster Type

Aghar are the lowest class in the Dwarven caste system — most Mountain Dwarves don't consider them part of any caste at all.

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| FREQUENCY | Rare |
| NO. APPEARING | 1–4 (2–20) |
| ARMOR CLASS | By armor type |
| MOVE | 12" |
| HIT DICE | Varies (1–4) |
| % IN LAIR | 45% |
| TREASURE TYPE | 25% chance of J |
| NO. OF ATTACKS | 1 or 2 |
| DAMAGE/ATTACK | By weapon type, or 1–4/1–4 (fist/bite) |
| SPECIAL ATTACKS | None |
| SPECIAL DEFENSES | **Save at 2 levels higher than actual HD** |
| MAGIC RESISTANCE | Standard |
| INTELLIGENCE | Low |
| ALIGNMENT | Chaotic Neutral |
| SIZE | S (3'–5') |
| PSIONIC ABILITY | Nil |
| XP VALUE | 14+1/hp; 28+2/hp; 50+3/hp; 85+4/hp |

**Appearance:** Raggedly clothed. Skin color from parchment to mottled to olive. Hair as unkempt as clothing. Generally bad health; bodies bear sores, scars, and calluses.

**Nature:** Though humans find them comical, Aghar are a disgusting race whose motto is "do anything, no matter how mean, to survive." Occasionally a decent, moral Aghar can be found — but very rare. Aghar believe magic is a sham that deserves to be exposed.

**Abilities:** Generally weak constitution and low intelligence but **above-average dexterity**. Despite their almost total inability to put two thoughts together: **excellent memories of all they see and hear** — making them a great source of raw, untapped information.

**Clan breakdown in this module:**
- **Glup Clan:** Primary gully dwarves serving the elevator; loyal to Phudge
- **Slud Clan:** Dragon's search party Aghar; barracks at 68c
- **Bulp Clan:** Aghar guards; Phudge's personal court guards at 68m

## SPECTRAL MINIONS — New Monster Type

Spectral Minions are the spirits of humans or demihumans who died before fulfilling powerful vows or quests they had undertaken.

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| FREQUENCY | Very Rare |
| NO. APPEARING | 1–40+ |
| ARMOR CLASS | 2 |
| MOVE | 30" (Restricted — see below) |
| HIT DICE | 3 (also see below) |
| % IN LAIR | 100% |
| TREASURE TYPE | Nil |
| NO. OF ATTACKS | 0 or 1 (see below) |
| DAMAGE/ATTACK | By weapon type (see below) |
| SPECIAL ATTACKS | None |
| SPECIAL DEFENSES | +1 or better weapon to hit |
| MAGIC RESISTANCE | 20% |
| INTELLIGENCE | Average |
| ALIGNMENT | Variable |
| SIZE | M |
| PSIONIC ABILITY | Nil |
| XP VALUE | 120 + 3/hp |

**Nature:** Do not exist fully on the Prime Material Plane. Remain bound to their vows or quests even in death. Every day they must relive the events leading to their deaths, trying to complete their mission.

**Range restriction:** Outdoors: must stay within 1,000 yards of the spot where they died. Indoors: within the fateful corridor or room.

**Initiative bonus:** Because of their speed, spectral minions have **+1 on initiative rolls**.

**Combat:** Only do damage if they died holding a weapon. The weapon becomes part of the spirit. Unless otherwise noted, only 50% of them have weapons. Those that do have swords unless otherwise noted.

**Removal:** Remove curse dispels spectral minions permanently. They also disappear forever if someone **fulfills their vow or quest**.

**Powerful individuals:** When very powerful people (such as high level paladins) become spectral minions, they retain the number of hit points they had before they died.

**Appearance:** Barely visible — colorless and transparent. Look very much as they did before death.

## KHISANTH (ONYX) — Ancient Huge Black Dragon

**Full designation:** Khisanth, an ancient, huge black dragon (also known as Onyx). Personal commander of the draconian forces at Xak Tsaroth; bound by duty to protect the Disks of Mishakal.

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| ALIGNMENT | Chaotic Evil |
| MOVE | 12"/24" (fly) |
| HIT POINTS | 64 |
| ARMOR CLASS | 3 |
| HIT DICE | 8 |
| NO. OF ATTACKS | 3 |
| DAMAGE/ATTACK | 1–4/1–4/3–18 (claw/claw/bite) |
| SPECIAL ATTACKS | Acid breath weapon; magic spells |
| BREATH WEAPON | Acid; damage = current HP total; save vs. dragon breath for half |

**Spell complement:**
- 1st Level (×8 slots): charm person ×2, magic missile ×2, shocking grasp ×2, sleep ×2

**Tactical behavior (surface encounter at 44k):**
1. Round 1: Gains speed in air; circles for better attack position
2. PCs subject to dragon fear (Monster Manual p. 30)
3. Attacks from air while hovering; avoids hand weapons
4. Bombards heroes with magic missile and sleep spells
5. Makes 2 aerial passes; uses breath weapon (full current HP) each pass
6. 3rd pass: flies over well, folds wings, drops like a stone into it; breaks out of dive far below; moves to lair

**Tactical behavior (underground at 67, 70j, 70k):**
- In area 67: Does not fight; gives orders to draconians, then retreats to lair
- In area 70j (if gong triggered): Pushes head through east doors, uses breath weapon; then calls for guards
- In area 70k: If she has breath weapon or spells, takes to air and uses them; otherwise fights on ground

**Reaction to Crystal Staff:** Hears the whisper from Mishakal and receives the instruction. A PC holding the staff while in the lair hears: "Retrieve the disks, and hit the dragon with this staff. All will be well." If a PC successfully hits Khisanth with the Crystal Staff, see the staff's destruction effect (DL1_08).
